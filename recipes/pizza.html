Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, olives, meat, etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)
  • Olive oil
  • Herbs (basil, oregano)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese and your choice of toppings.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle herbs on top.
  6. Bake in the oven for about 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
  7. Slice and serve hot.
